Rules

Welcome to CTF, Anwesha '16!

The event consists of teams of 3 members each competing against time and fellow hackers for ultimate glory and awesome prizes!

  • Contestants can register in teams with at most three members .
  • Login sessions are allowed only on a team basis.
  • Contestants have to complete the tasks that are !listed on their home page to secure points. The points vary according to the difficulty of the tasks. In the event of a tie, the total time difference between opening the tasks and solving them will be used as a tie breaker.
  • Teams with invalid Anwesha IDs or invalid user details stand to be disqualified
  • Any questionable activity that threatens the integrity and the smooth working of the event will result in a permanent ban.
  • The decision of the organizing team in all regards will be final and binding.
